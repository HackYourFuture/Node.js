-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                              Hello in TODO app Menu 
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1- list   : to show your TODO list.
2- add    : to add a new task to your list .
3- remove : to remove an old task from your list .
4- update : to update an existing task from your list .
5- reset  : < Warning > this commend will RESET your list !!.
6- exit   : to exit the app
